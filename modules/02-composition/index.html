Composition and Visual Framing

What You'll Be Able to Do

  • Frame a professional-looking interview.
  • Identify and correct distracting backgrounds.
  • Compose footage that is stable, intentional, and usable in an edit.
  • Make framing choices that support the final delivery format.

Module Assignment

Frame the same subject in a medium shot, medium close-up, and tight close-up. For each framing, note what changes about the background, the feeling of the shot, and the information it conveys.
