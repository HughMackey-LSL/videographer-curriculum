Module 7

Minimal Kit Operation

Become competent with the actual equipment you are likely to use in the field — so you can focus on the shoot, not the gear.

Lessons in This Module
1Camera and Lens→ 2Tripod, Gimbal, and Support→ 3Lighting Kit→ 4Audio Kit→ 5Power, Media, and Consumables→ 6Packing and Case Workflow→ ≡Module Quiz→

What You'll Be Able to Do

  • Pack the correct kit for a shoot.
  • Set up and tear down efficiently.
  • Identify missing or failed components before they compromise the shoot.
  • Operate the core kit without needing step-by-step supervision.

Module Assignment

Pack a kit for a hypothetical 60-minute interview shoot with lighting and audio, then time yourself setting up and tearing down. Note which items you forgot or had to search for, and what you would do differently.
