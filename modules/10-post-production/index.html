Post-Production Awareness

Understand what editors need so you can shoot with post-production in mind — making everyone's job easier downstream.

What You'll Be Able to Do

  • Shoot with post-production in mind.
  • Hand off media cleanly and with context.
  • Identify footage, audio, or coverage problems during self-review.
  • Understand how production decisions affect editing efficiency and final quality.

Module Assignment

Review footage from a recent shoot with an editor. Before the session, flag what you think worked and what didn't. During the session, listen for editor feedback. Write a brief summary of what helped and what hindered the edit — and what you would do differently next time.
