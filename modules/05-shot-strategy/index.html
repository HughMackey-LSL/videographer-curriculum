Module 5

Shot Strategy and Coverage

Think beyond simply recording what is happening — capture intentional, editable footage for interviews, events, and general production needs.

Lessons in This Module

What You'll Be Able to Do

  • Plan and capture useful footage for interviews, events, and general assignments.
  • Collect footage that supports an edit rather than merely documenting activity.
  • Anticipate key moments and visual needs without a shot-by-shot script.
  • Return with enough variety for a short finished piece, recap, or archival record.

Module Assignment

Shoot a five-shot sequence of someone performing a familiar task (making coffee, setting up equipment, walking into a room). Use a wide, medium, close-up, detail, and reaction shot. Then capture B-roll for a hypothetical one-minute recap video on the same topic.
