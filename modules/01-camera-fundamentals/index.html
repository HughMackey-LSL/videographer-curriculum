Camera Fundamentals

Make the camera predictable and controllable before adding the complexity of interviews, events, lighting, or audio.

What You'll Be Able to Do

  • Set up a camera from scratch.
  • Select appropriate resolution, frame rate, shutter speed, aperture, ISO, and white balance.
  • Capture a properly exposed, focused, and color-balanced image.
  • Avoid common beginner errors such as incorrect frame rate, bad white balance, soft focus, overexposure, and underexposure.

Module Assignment

Set up a camera, white balance, expose, and focus correctly in three different rooms with different lighting conditions. Compare your results and note what adjustments each location required.
